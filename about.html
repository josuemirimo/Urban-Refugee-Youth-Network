Our Story

The founders of Urban Refugee Youth Network (URYN), together with the executive committee, established this youth-led organization to build initiatives that identify, nurture, promote, and improve the well-being of refugee and host community youth.

Through advocacy, capacity-building, and service provision, we foster collaborations and empower youth to contribute positively to their communities. We believe every youth deserves education, career, and financial responsibility that supports their physical and emotional well-being.

URYN is committed to creating a supportive and understanding host community, free from discrimination, and guided by respect for all. Our constitution is the foundation for our work and our future.
