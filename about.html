Our Story

The founders of Urban Refugee Youth Network (URYN), together with the executive committee, established this youth-led organization to build initiatives that identify, nurture, promote, and improve the well-being of refugee and host community youth. Through advocacy, capacity-building, and service provision, we foster collaborations and empower youth to contribute positively to their communities. We believe every youth deserves education, career, and financial responsibility that supports their physical and emotional well-being. URYN is committed to creating a supportive and understanding host community, free from discrimination, and guided by respect for all. Our constitution is the foundation for our work and our future.

Our Mission

To build initiatives that identify, nurture, promote, and improve the well-being of refugee and host community youth through advocacy, capacity-building, and service provision. We foster collaborations, empower youth, and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Vision

To contribute towards a more supportive and understanding host community that recognizes and values the potential of refugees.

Our Core Values

01

Transparency & Accountability

We operate with openness and integrity, ensuring our actions and decisions are clear and responsible. Accountability is central to building trust with our youth, partners, and communities.

02

Restoration of Hope

We inspire and restore hope among refugee youth, helping them envision a brighter future and overcome adversity through opportunity, support, and encouragement.

03

Equal Consideration of All People

We treat every individual with fairness and respect, regardless of background, race, or belief. Inclusion and equal opportunity are at the heart of our work.

04

Humanitarianism

Our actions are guided by compassion and a commitment to improving lives. We respond to needs with empathy and strive to make a positive impact in every community we serve.

05

Respect & Dignity

We honor the culture, beliefs, and choices of all people, fostering an environment where everyone is valued and treated with dignity.

06

Gender Equality

We promote equal rights and opportunities for all genders, empowering youth to challenge stereotypes and build inclusive communities.

07

Collaboration & Positive Change

We work together with stakeholders, partners, and youth to drive positive change, believing that collective action leads to stronger, more resilient communities.

Our Objectives

  • Provide essential services such as education, healthcare, psychosocial support, and livelihood opportunities to youth.
  • Empower youth through skills training, capacity building, leadership development, and access to resources.
  • Advocate for the rights and interests of refugee youth at local, national, and international levels.
  • Foster a sense of belonging and community among refugee youth through networking, cultural exchange, and recreational activities.
