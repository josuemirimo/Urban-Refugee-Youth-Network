About URYNet

Urban Refugee Youth Network (URYNet) is a youth-led initiative uniting diverse nationalities to build skills, income, and peaceful coexistence in Kampala’s urban context.

Mission

Building initiatives that identify, nurture, and promote refugee potentials.

Vision

A supportive host community that recognizes refugee talent and potential.
