Refugee Youth Festival 2025

Published on July 20, 2025

The Refugee Youth Festival 2025 brought together young people, community leaders, and partners in Kampala for a celebration of culture, resilience, and youth empowerment. The festival showcased art, music, entrepreneurship, and sports as powerful tools for peace and inclusion.

Participants shared inspiring stories of change, while exhibitions highlighted youth-led businesses and community projects. The event not only celebrated achievements but also created new opportunities for networking and collaboration between refugees and host communities.

Urban Refugee Youth Network remains committed to building pathways to self-reliance for refugee youth through education, skills training, health, livelihoods, and cultural initiatives.
